Why are they Rivals?

Though they are friends, Professor X and Magneto have differeing opinions on how to integreate in the world as Mutants.

Professor X wants Mutants to use their special abilities to help the world and humans, so both species can be cordial and thrive in the same environment.

Magneto on the otherhand, has a distrust with humans and wants to protect his own people by using their mutant abilities to enhance their own lives and achieving as musch as possible, by any means necessary.

Therefore, these friends/foes clash over their ideas often. Professor X along with the X-Men, try to stop Magneto and his band of mutants from wreaking havoc amongst humans.
